HELLO, I'M NOOR

Welcome to Noor, your local real-time translator designed to convert Arabic & English text into Braille. Noor aims to enhance accessibility for visually impaired individuals by providing a seamless translation experience. With Noor, reading and understanding has never been easier.

WHAT'S NOOR

Noor is a groundbreaking project dedicated to improving access to information for visually impaired individuals. At its core, Noor is a real-time translation device that converts Arabic text into Braille using the ESP32 microcontroller. The project aims to provide an accessible and reliable solution for educational institutions, public spaces, and personal use. Noor's mission is to empower visually impaired users by making written information easily accessible and to foster greater independence and inclusion.
